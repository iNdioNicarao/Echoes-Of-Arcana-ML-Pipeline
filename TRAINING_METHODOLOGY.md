# Echoes Arcana: Custom Training & Lore Building Guide

This document outlines the strategy for training the local MLX Qwen model (and fallback cloud models) to perfect the "Voice of the Oracle" and incorporate deep esoteric lore (Astrology, Numerology) into its readings.

## Core Training Methods

### 1. LoRA Fine-Tuning (Local MLX)
Instead of relying entirely on heavy system prompts, we use Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) fine-tuning to physically bake the Oracle's voice and behavior into the model's weights.
* **Objective:** Teach the model to naturally use esoteric vocabulary, avoid robotic transitions (e.g., "Card 1 means...", "In conclusion"), and understand the structural flow of a mystical reading.
* **Artifacts:** `FineTuning/generate_tarot_dataset.py`, `train.jsonl`, and the `adapters/` directory.

### 2. Advanced System Prompting
While fine-tuning handles the "vibe," the system prompt (`oracle_system_prompt.txt`) acts as the strict rulebook.
* **Objective:** Force the model to adhere to specific constraints, such as explicit card synthesis (how cards interact) and acknowledging the Querent's past journal notes to make readings feel continuous.

### 3. Dynamic Archetype Injection
To keep the Oracle engaging and prevent the voice from becoming monotonous, we inject specific archetypes into the prompt based on context triggers:
* **The Trickster (5% chance):** Mischievous, paradoxical readings.
* **The Shadow Weaver:** Triggered by multiple reversed cards late at night (12 AM - 4 AM) for a darker, cryptic tone.
* **The Empathic Comfort vs. The Stern Warning:** Injected based on the presence of heavy cards (The Tower, 3 of Swords) or deceptive cards (The Devil).

### 4. TTS (Text-to-Speech) Persona Tuning
The audible voice generated by `Qwen3_TTS` is tuned to match the text archetypes. By adjusting the `VoiceProfile` (pitch, rate, speaker prompt), the spoken audio mirrors the generated text's energy (e.g., a "breathy Celtic lilt" for standard readings, or a harsher tone for The Guardian).

## Expanding the Lore Dataset (Astrology & Numerology)

The foundational logic for Astrology and Numerology already exists in the `EchoesArcana` codebase. The next step is to train the model to *synthesize* this data dynamically.

### 1. Astrological Synthesis
* **Current State:** The `AstrologyService.swift` calculates the Querent's Sun, Moon, and Rising signs and injects them into the prompt. Tarot cards also have an `astrology` property.
* **Training Goal:** Teach the model to recognize clashes or harmonies. (e.g., *“Your Taurus Moon craves stability, yet The Tower demands you surrender it.”* or *“Your watery Cancer nature is at odds with the fiery Page of Wands.”*)

### 2. Numerology and Card Interactions
* **Current State:** The data generator includes a `NUMEROLOGY` dictionary mapping numbers 1-10 to concepts.
* **Training Goal:** Train the Oracle to notice patterns. If a spread contains multiple cards of the same number, the Oracle should explicitly call it out. (e.g., *“The resonance of the number Two echoes throughout your spread—you are standing at a crossroads of duality.”*)

### 3. Kabbalistic Elements
* **Training Goal:** Train the Oracle to recognize elemental conflicts based on suits (Wands/Fire, Cups/Water, Swords/Air, Pentacles/Earth). (e.g., *“Your fiery ambition is being drowned out by deep emotional undercurrents.”*)

## Structuring the 1,000-Reading Dataset

To properly train the model to handle all these esoteric concepts without losing focus on the Tarot, the 1,000 accumulated readings should be structured as a blended dataset:

* **700 Readings (The Core):** Standard readings focusing purely on the cards, upright/reversed meanings, and answering the user's intent in the Oracle's voice.
* **100 Readings (Astrological Synthesis):** The Oracle explicitly references the Querent's Sun/Moon/Rising signs and how they interact with the drawn cards.
* **100 Readings (Numerology/Patterns):** The Oracle notices multiples of the same number in a spread or highlights elemental imbalances.
* **100 Readings (Follow-up & Journaling):** The Oracle specifically addresses the user's past journal entries and acknowledges previous readings.

**Alternative Approach (Expansion):** If the initial 1,000 standard readings are already complete, we can generate an additional 500 readings heavily focused on Astrology and Numerology to append to `train.jsonl`. The fine-tuning process will average out the behaviors, teaching the model to weave in planetary alignments and numbers naturally when the context is provided.
